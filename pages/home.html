About us

Welcome to our blog! We like to play a little game every time we go shopping and this blog is to bring you guys with us.

Every time my girlfriend would take me out shopping, I played a little game to keep my self entertained. I would try to show her the ugliest thing. After a couple of shopping sessions, she joined in as well. The best part of our game was that when one of us thought that they had the worst they would be one-upped by the other.

Game Rules

The game is simple. There are a couple of categories and you can only vote once on each. The categories are men's and women's shoes, jewelry, shirts, and women's bags.
